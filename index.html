How to cook a delicious Jollof Rice

There are many different ways to cook Jollof rice It is a dish made with;

  • Rice

  • Fresh tomatoes

  • Sachet/Tin tomato paste

  • Scotch bonnet peppers

  • Salt and Spices

If your jollof rice dosen't have the above mentioned ingredients, then it's not a true jollof. I usually make it the way my mom taught me, and i usually call it Nigerian jollof because of the type of rice that is used. Nigerians use the lond grain rice that has been parboiled. other versions i.e Ghanaian jollof will use basmati or jasmine rice.

Tips on cooking the best jollof rie

  1. It is best to use parboiled rice so that you don't end up with mushy rice.

  2. When cooking the tomato stew, you can tell when it's done because the oil will rise to the top. It will be a deep red color and the bitter taste of the tomato has been cooked out

  3. Reserve 1/4th of the tomato mixture and add it at end to help prevent the rice from burning. Also, it gives the rice that beautiful red color we all know and Love.

  4. Cover the top of the pot with plastic wrap before putting the lid on to help keep all the steam in the pot.
